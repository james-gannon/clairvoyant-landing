About

Clairvoyant is a mobile app that allows anyone to capture, combine and share unique virtual objects that are hidden in the real world.

In the app, a player will be able to Use their AR camera to capture common, rare and legendary 3D objects randomly dropped outside. Once an object is captured, XP is added to the player's profile.
